Clever:
1:	a: Skillful or adroit in using the hands or body : nimble.
		- Ex: Clever fingers.
	b: Mentally quick and resourceful.
		- Ex: A clever young lawyer.

2:	Marked by wit or ingenuity.
	- Ex: A clever solution.
	- Ex: A clever idea. 
	- Ex: The play's clever dialogue.

3:	Dialect	a: Good
			b: Easy to use or handle.
